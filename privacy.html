Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 December 2025

Thank you for using Calm Down Quotes. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We do not ask you to provide personal information such as your name, email address, or contact details to use the website.

The website automatically collects limited, **non-personally identifiable** information for analytics and basic security purposes, such as:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type
  • Anonymous usage data (for example, page views and interactions)

2. Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies or similar technologies for the purpose of operating and analyzing the use of the website (via Google Analytics, see Section 4). These help us understand how the website is used and to improve performance and user experience.

3. How We Use Information

Any anonymous or aggregated data collected helps us to:

  • Improve website performance and reliability
  • Understand which content is most useful
  • Improve user experience and feature development

4. Third-Party Services

We use **Google Analytics 4 (GA4)** to collect the anonymous usage data mentioned in Section 1. This service uses cookies and is configured with **IP anonymization** to ensure data collection meets privacy standards. Google's use of this information is governed by their own privacy policies. We do not use any other third-party tracking services or advertising networks.

5. Your Choices

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality. For more information on how Google Analytics collects and processes data, please visit How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above reflects the latest version.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may reach out through any contact method provided on the website (if available).
